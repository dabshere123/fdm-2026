🔐 How to Log In

Open fdm2026.netlify.app/field on your phone. No app download needed.

1
Enter Your Name
Type your full name exactly as it appears on your staff registration. Case doesn't matter.
2
Select Your Role
Choose from the dropdown. If you work multiple roles across days, log out and back in with the correct role for your shift.
3
Enter Your Location
Type where you're stationed — e.g. "Moon Bar", "Sun Stage Left", "Medical Tent". This auto-fills your location on all calls you submit.
4
Tap Sign In
You're in. Add the page to your home screen for faster access next time.
💡 Add to home screen: tap the Share button in Safari → "Add to Home Screen" — it opens full-screen with no browser bar.

👤 Roles & What They See

Admin 1 Admin 2 Med 1 Med 2 Bar Staff Stage Manager Hospitality Overnight

Admin 1 / Admin 2

Full access to the Hub. See all active calls, manage staff, access MPD officers, send broadcasts, manage Lost & Found, run end-of-night reports.

Med 1 / Med 2

Access to Med Hub. See all medical and fire calls, manage walk-in patients, run incident reports, access the 911 activation.

All Other Roles

Worker App only. Can submit calls, report lost children, log L&F items, chat, and receive broadcast alerts. The app automatically routes your calls to the right team.

🚨 Submitting Any Call

All calls follow the same pattern regardless of type.

1
Tap the call type button
From your home screen: Medical, Fire/Life Safety, Security, or Supplies. Lost Child has its own dedicated button.
2
Confirm or update location
Your login location auto-fills. Update it if the incident is elsewhere — be specific: "Moon Stage left side near barricade" is better than "Moon Stage".
3
Describe the situation
Keep it brief and factual: "Guest down, unresponsive" or "Smell of smoke near stage left" or "Aggressive patron refusing to leave".
4
Tap Submit
Admin, Med team, and relevant units are notified by SMS and the Hub immediately. Stay at the scene until help arrives.
⚠️ For life-threatening emergencies — always call 911 directly first. The app alerts festival staff, not 911.

🩺 Medical Call

1
Tap the Medical button
Purple button on home screen. Opens the call form.
2
Location + description
Examples: "Guest unconscious near Moon Bar entrance" · "Patron having seizure near stage right" · "Heat exhaustion, north lawn"
3
Submit — stay with patient
Med team is alerted by SMS and voice call. Keep the scene clear, note any changes in the patient's condition to report to medics.
💡 Mention any visible AED location nearby: "AED on north wall of Moon Stage."

🔥 Fire / Life Safety

⚠️ Call 911 immediately if there is actual fire or smoke. Then submit the call in the app.
1
Call 911 first
Never rely on the app alone for fire. Call 911, then open the app.
2
Tap Fire / Life Safety
Describe what you see: "Smoke from backstage area, Sun Stage" · "Electrical smell, Lagniappe Bar back room" · "Gas smell near vendor row"
3
Begin crowd movement
If safe to do so, calmly direct guests away from the area. Do not cause panic. Follow directions from Admin Hub.

🛡️ Security Call

1
Tap Security
Blue button. Use for: aggressive patrons, fights, theft, threats, crowd control issues, anything that needs a security response.
2
Describe specifically
"Male patron, red shirt, threatening bartender at Moon Bar" · "Fight in progress, north lawn near vendor row" · "Unauthorized person backstage, Sun Stage"
3
Do not intervene physically
Unless you are trained security — stay visible and watch. Note descriptions. Security team and MPD are alerted.
ℹ️ MPD officers can be requested directly from the Hub if needed. On-duty officers receive an SMS immediately.

📦 Supplies Request

1
Tap Supplies
For requesting bar restocks, ice, napkins, cups, or any operational supply from a bar station or stage.
2
Be specific about what you need
"Moon Bar needs 2 bags ice and more Spotted Cow" · "Lafayette Bar — out of 16oz cups" · "Sun Stage Right — need more barricade pins"
3
Submit and wait
Your request routes to Admin. Response typically comes via chat or someone shows up. Check chat for updates.

🧒 Lost Child

Two different scenarios — tap the right one.

🚨 MISSING CHILD — A child is lost

A parent/guardian tells you their child is missing, or you notice an unaccompanied child who appears distressed.

1
Tap Lost Child → Missing Child
Opens a form for child details.
2
Fill out the form
Name: if known · Age: approximate is fine · Hair: color and style · Clothing: be specific — color, type · Last seen: where/with whom · Guardian: name of person reporting
3
Submit — PA script appears
All staff are alerted immediately. You'll see a PA announcement script ready to hand to the Stage Manager for the announcer to read over the loudspeaker.
4
Hand script to Stage Manager
Tap "Copy Script" and send it to the nearest Stage Manager. They'll give it to the announcer. You can also read it to them over radio.

🧒✅ CHILD FOUND HERE — You have a lost child

A child has been brought to your location, or you found an unaccompanied child.

1
Tap Lost Child → Child Found Here
Opens a form to describe the child and your location.
2
Fill out the form
Name: if the child told you · Age: approximate · Hair & Clothing: as specific as possible · Your location: where you are right now — this goes in the PA announcement · Guardian: if they mentioned a name
3
Submit — PA script appears
All staff are notified. A PA script is generated directing parents to your location. Copy and hand it to the Stage Manager.
4
Stay with the child
Never leave the child alone. Keep them calm. Admin or a med team member will come to you. Do not release the child to anyone without Admin confirmation.
🚨 Never leave a child alone under any circumstances. Stay with them until Admin or security arrives.

📋 Lost & Found

Someone turned in an item, or you found something.

1
Tap Lost & Found on home screen
Opens the L&F logging form.
2
Take a photo
Tap the camera area to take a photo of the item. This is required — it helps identify items when people come to claim them.
3
Fill in item details
Description: "Black iPhone 14 with cracked screen" · "Blue Hydro Flask, green lid" · "Car keys, Toyota fob, blue lanyard"
4
Where found & day
Be specific: "Moon Bar, near east exit" — this determines the item's tag number format.
5
Submit — write the tag number on the item
You'll see a tag number like SAT-MOON-042. Write it on a piece of tape and attach it to the item, then place the item in the L&F box.
💡 The L&F lookup page is at fdm2026.netlify.app/lostfound — guests can search by tag number, item description, or where it was found.
ℹ️ Tag format: DAY-LOCATION-### (e.g. THU-SUNL-007 = Thursday, Sun Stage Left, item 7)

💬 Festival Chat

1
Tap Chat on home screen
Opens your channel list — you see channels relevant to your role.
2
Tap a channel to open it
All Staff, your specific station channel, or direct message with another staff member.
3
Type your message and send
Chat is not for emergencies — use the call buttons for that. Chat is for coordination: "Lafayette Bar running low on cups" or "Anyone have a walkie charger?"
💡 A notification banner appears at the top of your home screen when a new message arrives. Tap it to reply quickly without leaving the home screen.

📣 Broadcast Alerts

Sent from Admin Hub to all or specific staff groups.

What you'll see

A colored banner appears at the top of your home screen. Red = emergency. Orange = urgent. Blue = information. Yellow = weather. Tap it to dismiss or reply.

Types of broadcasts

🚨 Emergency All-Call · 🌩️ Weather Alert · ⚡ Power Outage · 🎺 Stage Change · 📦 Restock Request · 💬 General Announcement

💡 Some broadcasts require acknowledgment — you'll see a green ACK button. Tap it so admin knows you received the message.

⚙️ Admin & Med Hub

For Admin 1, Admin 2, Med 1, Med 2 only. Access at fdm2026.netlify.app/hub

Safety Section

911 Activation — alerts all festival units. YOU must call 911 separately.

New Call — submit Medical, Fire, or Security call from the hub. Security shows MPD sub-section: Request MPD Officers (SMS + voice call to on-duty officers) and Manage Officers.

Report Lost Child — same lost child form as the worker app.

Manage Lost & Found

View all logged items, filter by day, mark items as Claimed, move to Fest Office, or add items directly from the hub.

Send Onboarding Text

Select a staff member from the dropdown — their phone auto-fills from Airtable. Sends them the app link and role confirmation via SMS.

Broadcast

Send an alert to all staff or specific groups. Select the type (Emergency, Weather, Stage Change, etc.), write the message, and send. Appears instantly on all worker home screens.

MPD Officers (Admin only)

Add officers with name, phone, badge number, and shift schedule. Set status to Online when they arrive. When you request MPD from the Security call form, all Online officers receive an SMS and voice call simultaneously.

Staff List & Onboarding

View all registered staff. Filter by role or status. Send individual onboarding texts or resend to all. Change staff status to Approved to trigger the automatic onboarding text.

End of Night Report

Run from the hub at the end of each night. Summarizes all calls, incidents, and L&F items. Sent to admin by email/SMS.

ℹ️ Med Hub shows the same safety section as Admin Hub, plus walk-in patient management and incident reporting for medical calls.

⚡ Quick Tips

📱 Add the worker app to your phone's home screen for full-screen access without the browser bar. (Safari → Share → Add to Home Screen)
🔋 Keep your phone charged. Bring a portable charger if possible. The app works offline for reading but needs connection to submit calls.
📍 Be specific with locations. "Moon Bar" is good. "Moon Bar, near the east gate, middle of the crowd" is better. The more specific, the faster help arrives.
🧒 If you encounter a lost child, your location in the PA script is exactly what you typed in the "Your Location" field. Make it count.
📋 L&F tag goes on the physical item AND stays in the app. Anyone can look up an item at fdm2026.netlify.app/lostfound.
💬 Use chat for coordination. Use the call buttons for incidents. Don't report an emergency in chat — it won't alert the right people.
