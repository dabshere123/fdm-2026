📲 Submitting a Request
When something needs attention, open the app and tap the relevant request type. Operations will be notified immediately by text, voice call, and GroupMe.
🚨
Medical Emergency
Any medical situation — injury, illness, unresponsive patron
🛡
Security
Altercation, threat, patron removal needed
🔥
Fire / Life Safety
Fire, hazard, or life safety concern
📦
Supplies / Restock
Ice, cups, napkins, supplies running low
🔧
Maintenance
Equipment failure, generator, structural issue
Once your request is acknowledged, you'll receive a text confirmation. You'll receive another text when the issue is resolved.
🧒 Lost Child Protocol
If you encounter a lost child, act immediately. Do not leave the child unattended.
1
Tap "Report Lost Child" in the Worker App.
2
Fill in the details — child's description, last known location, and assembly point.
3
Read the radio script that appears on your screen word for word over your venue's PA system.
4
All staff are alerted immediately by text and voice call. Keep the child calm and stay with them until operations arrives.
