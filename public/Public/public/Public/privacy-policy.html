Fête de Marquette Operations

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

This privacy policy describes how Fête de Marquette Operations collects, uses, and protects the personal information of festival staff and volunteers who use the Fête de Marquette Operations app and related communications.

Information We Collect

When staff register for the operations app, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Mobile phone number
  • Role and assigned location within the festival
  • Working days and shift times

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for festival operations purposes, including:

  • Sending operational alerts, safety notifications, and staff communications via SMS and voice call during the event
  • Equipment check-in and check-out tracking
  • Shift reminders and event updates
  • Emergency communications

SMS and Voice Communications

By registering for the Fête de Marquette Operations app, you consent to receive SMS messages and voice calls from Fête de Marquette Operations related to your role at the festival. Message frequency varies based on event activity. Standard message and data rates may apply.

To opt out of SMS communications, reply STOP to any message. To opt back in, reply START. For help, reply HELP.

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your information may be shared with:

  • Twilio Inc. — our SMS and voice communications provider, solely for the purpose of delivering messages
  • Festival operations staff who need access to coordinate the event

Data Retention

Registration data is retained for the duration of the festival and deleted within 30 days after the event concludes. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting festival operations.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. The operations app is password-protected and access is limited to authorized festival staff.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how your data is used, please contact Fête de Marquette Operations directly at the festival office or through your event coordinator.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this URL.
